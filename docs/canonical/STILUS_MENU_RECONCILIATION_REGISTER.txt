RR-002
====================================================================

Title:
Revision Marks Ownership

Status:
OPEN

Current Situation:

Revision Marks appear in both:

Navigate
Revise

Proposed Resolution:

Revise

    Mark Revision [REV]
    Mark Citation Needed [CITE]
    Mark Check [CHECK]
    Clear All Marks

    Next Mark
    Previous Mark

Reason:

Creation belongs to revision.

Movement belongs to navigation.

====================================================================

Title:
Options Menu Removal

Status:
OPEN

Current Situation:

Options contains too few commands to justify a dedicated menu.

Proposed Resolution:

Remove:

Options

Research
