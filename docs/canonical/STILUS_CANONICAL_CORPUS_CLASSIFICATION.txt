STILUS CANONICAL CORPUS CLASSIFICATION

Status: CANONICAL
Phase: P8-015

Purpose:

Classify the status of every canonical document.

This document prevents endless rewriting of historical material.

Documents must be classified as:

ACTIVE
FROZEN
SUPERSEDED
ARCHIVED
HISTORICAL

====================================================================
STATUS DEFINITIONS
====================================================================

ACTIVE

Current authoritative document.
May evolve through audit and decision.

----------------------------------------------------

FROZEN

Authoritative baseline.

Modification requires:

Audit
Finding
Decision

----------------------------------------------------

SUPERSEDED

Replaced by a newer authoritative document.

Retained for historical traceability.

----------------------------------------------------

ARCHIVED

Working document whose mission is completed.

Retained for audit history.

----------------------------------------------------

HISTORICAL

Historical audit or evidence document.

Never rewritten.

CORPUS RULE
====================================================================

Historical documents are not rewritten.

Superseded documents are not rewritten.

Archived documents are not rewritten.

Only ACTIVE documents may evolve.

FROZEN documents require formal reopening.
